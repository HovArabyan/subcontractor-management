Competitive Position

BuildOps has a head start on AP/billing depth — retainage, CCOs, and a dedicated Subcontract object are all live today and won't be in ST until P1. They also have polished outbound document workflows (branded PDFs, RBAC).

ServiceTitan wins on integration breadth — SA costing, CommHub two-way threading, native dispatch, and the Sub Spend Report are things BuildOps structurally cannot replicate. The P1/P2 roadmap (portal, AI-assisted selection, performance ratings, loginless forms) is more ambitious than anything BuildOps has signaled.
